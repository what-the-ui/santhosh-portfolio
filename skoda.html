Škoda Connected App

In-car connectivity app redesigned for seamless interaction between driver and vehicle — a smarter, safer, more intuitive experience.

Cover image coming soon
Overview

Škoda connected app is an in-vehicle and companion mobile app that allows drivers to interact with their car remotely and on the go. I redesigned the full iOS and Android experience to reduce cognitive load while driving, simplify remote vehicle controls, and create a cohesive visual identity aligned with the Škoda brand.

Role
Manager, Product Design
Platform
iOS · Android
Year
2023
Team
Design · Product · Engineering
The Problem

"Drivers found the existing app confusing and distracting to use — too many steps to perform basic actions like locking the car or checking range, with a UI that felt generic rather than premium."

Process & Solution
01
Research
Understanding users, pain points, and market context.
  • User interviews with buyers & sellers
  • Competitive analysis across resale platforms
  • Analytics review of drop-off funnels
  • Heuristic evaluation of current app
02
Define
Synthesising insights into clear problem statements.
  • Affinity mapping of research findings
  • User journey mapping
  • Defining core jobs-to-be-done
  • Prioritisation with product team
03
Design
Exploring, iterating, and refining solutions.
  • Information architecture restructure
  • Low-fi wireframes & flows
  • High-fidelity UI in Figma
  • Prototype testing & iteration
04
Deliver
Shipping and measuring impact.
  • Design handoff & specs
  • QA collaboration with engineers
  • A/B testing on key flows
  • Post-launch metrics review
